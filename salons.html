About
Known for possessing a fresh, uncontrived approach to hair and beauty, Berlin Friseure are the session stylists of choice to New Zealand's top fashion designers, directors and photographers.

Their status as the industry arbiter is firmly cemented amongst a generation of bright young things – a result of their considered approach to the creative process, education, sustainability and the environment.
